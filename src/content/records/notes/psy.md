## Research Methods

1. Main approaches 流派
2. Research method
    - Non-experimental method
        - Observation
            - Naturalistic Observation
                - Limitation: Hawthorne effect 霍桑效应，知道自己被观察会影响行为
            - Laboratory Observation
        - Case study 个案研究法, one participant or a small group
            - Advantage: the tremendous amount of detail it provides
            - Disadvantage: researcher can't really apply the result to other similar people
        - Survey 调查法, 使用 Likert scales 量化抽象概念, 方便统计分析
            - Advantages
                - Get private informtation
                - Get a tremendous amount on a very large group
                - It is not limited by time or space
            - Disadvantage
                - People aren't always going to give researchers accurate answers (social desirability bias 社会期望偏差)
        - Interview 访谈: face-to-face or by telephone
            - Structured interview: 问题固定
            - Unstructured interview: 问题不固定: flexible but hard to compare data
            - Semi-structured interview
        - Meta-analysis 元分析: a statistical combination of result from individual studies 用多个相似实验进行分析, analysis about analysis 对分析进行分析
            - Advantages
                - Can summarize the results from individual studies
                - Combines data from individual studies, obtain larger sample size
            - Disadvantages
                - Combines different types of studies, the summary effect may ignore important differences between studies
                - In some field, studies are too heterogeneous to be comparable 不同研究差异过大，无法比较
        - Correlation studies 相关研究
            - Correlation coefficient 相关系数 $r$
            - Disadvantage: cannot generate cause and effect relationship
    - Experimental method
        - Advantages
            - To determine the cause of a behavior
            - Precise enough
        - Disadvantages
            - Low external promotion (external validity 外部效度)
            - Generalizable?
        - True experiment: IV can be manipulated 可被控制
        - 要素
            - Aims
            - Variables
                - Independent variable (IV) 自变量
                - Dependent variable (DV) 因变量
                - Irrelevant variable: confounding/extraneous variable 混淆变量
            - Operational definition 操作性定义: 有明确数字
                - How to measure
                - 提高实验可重复性
            - Hypothesis: a testable statement
                - Non-directional (two-tailed) hypothesis: IV will change DV
                - Directional (one-tailed) hypothesis: IV will increase/decrease DV
                - Null hypothesis: there is no difference in the DV between X and Y
            - Population: 希望推广到的最大人群
            - Sample: represent the population
                - Representative → generalizable results
            - Random assignment / experimental manipulation
                - Control participant difference
            - Pair review 同行评审
            - Replication
        - Disadvantages
            - Experimenter effect 实验者效应 (Rosenthal Effect / Pygmalion Effect): expectations, 无意中给被试暗示
                - 解决: Double Blind
            - Demand characteristic 需求特性 (Hawthorne Effect / Social Desirablity Bias): 被试知道实验目标之后行为变化
                - 解决: Single blind
            - Placebo effect 安慰剂效应
                - 解决: 安慰剂组
3. Validity and reliability
    1. Validity 效度: accuracy, the degree to which a measuring tool or instrument can accurately measure what needs to be measured
        1. Construct validity 结构效度：测验包含内容的完整程度
            1. 评价：专家评价
        2. Predictive validity 预测效度
    2. Reliability 信度: consistency, the degree of consistency in the results of repeated measurements of the same object using the same method
        1. Test-retest reliability 重测信度：重复测试结果相似程度
        2. Split-half reliability 分半信度
    3. 信度不高，效度一定不高，但效度不高，信度可能很高
4. Ethical guidelines in psychology
    - Rights and well-being
    - Informed consent 知情同意 (未成年需要监护人同意)
    - No coercion 非强迫
    - Little risk 最小风险
    - Debrief 事后告知
    - Anonymity or Confidentiality
    - Local institutional review board (IRB)
    - (Animals) Avoiding any unneccessary pain or suffering
5. Types of data
    - Qualitative: descriptive, in-depth results
    - Quantitative
        - 量化方法: Likert scales
6. Types of statistics
    - Descriptive Statistics 描述性统计
        - Central tendencies
            - Median
            - Mean
            - Mode
            - Distributions
                - Bimodal
                - Normal
                - Positive skew / skewed right
                - Negative skew / skewed left
        - Measures of variability
            - Range
            - Standard deviation
            - z-score
    - Inferential Statistics 推断性统计
        - Significant difference 统计学显著 (experiment 与 control 确实有差别)
        - Effect size 效应量 (相差多少)
    - Regression toward the mean (RTM) 均值回归: 大数定律

2. Difference threshold / just noticable differences (JND) 差别感受阈限
    - Weber's law: $K = \cfrac{\Delta I}{I}$, 其中 $K$ 是常数，$I$ 是 initial stimulus
3. Synesthesia 联觉: 一种刺激，两种感受
4. Sensory transduction: 转化为神经信号
5. Sensory interaction 感觉交互: 两种感觉相互影响
6. Visual anatomy
    1. Structure of eye
        - Lens 晶状体: focus the light rays falling on the retina; accomodation 聚焦
        - Pupil 瞳孔: pass into the rear chamber of the eye
        - Retina 视网膜: absorbs light, processes images, and sends visual information to the brain
            - Cones 视锥细胞: daylight vision and color vision; 少
            - Rods 视杆细胞: night vision and peripheral vision; 多
            - Light adaptation
            - Dark adaptation
            - Fovea 中央凹: greatest visual acuity 敏度
    2. Blind spot 盲点
    3. Optic chiasm 视交叉神经
    4. Parallel processing 平行加工
