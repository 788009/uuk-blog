## Unit 1 - Basics of Economics

1. Scarcity
    1. 有限的资源无法满足无限的需求 -> 研究如何分配资源
    2. Choices Trade off 三大基本问题
        - What to produce
        - How to produce
        - For whom to produce
2. Factor of production 生产要素
    - Labor
    - Capital 资本
        -  Physical
        -  Human
    - Natural resources / land
    - Entrepreneurship 企业家精神
3. Economic system
    - Command (planned) economy
        - Government decide resources allocation
        - Public property right 产权/所有权归政府所有
        - Pack of incentive for profit -> less efficient
    - Market (capitalism) economy
        - Market price determine resource allocation
        - Private property right
        - Freedom of exchange, competition and profit incentive -> more efficient -> less equality
    - Mixed economy: mixed of command system and market system
        - interaction of buyers and sellers in markets
4. Opportunity cost: what you give upto get an item / the next best alternative that forgone 放弃的下一个备选项的价值
    - Explicit cost 显性: 有支出（如学费）
    - Inplicit cost 隐性: 无支出但有放弃（如快乐、经验）
5. Production possibility curves (PPC): shows the maximum quantity of one good that can be produced for each possible quantity of the other good produced, given the economy's resource and technology
    1. Points
        - The economy can produce any combination **on or inside the frontier**.
        - Points **on PPC** represent efficient levels of production.
        - Points **inside the frontier** represent an inefficient outcome.
        - Points **outside the frontier** are not feasible (attainable given the economy's resources)
    2. Curves
        - 直线: 机会成本固定；两个产品需要的原料一模一样 (adaptable)
        - 曲线：机会成本递增
        - 多为 concave to origin / bowed out
    3. Shift of PPC
        1. Factors
            - Changes in resources
            - Changes in technology and productivity
        2. Types
            - Economy growth: PPC moves outward
            - Negative economy growth: PPC moves inward
            - 某个产品的上限增加: 只用于该产品的原料增加
            - 多产 capital goods 比多产 consumer goods 更能产生未来的 economy growth
6. Absolute advantage and comparative advantage
    1. Absolute advantage
        - Input: 比谁少
        - Output: 比谁多
    2. Comparative advantage: lower opportunity cost
        - Trade can be mutually beneficial -> consider comparative advantage
        - If has comparative advantage -> specialize
        - With trade, both can consume a point beyond its PPC
7. Terms of Trade 贸易条件
    1. Price
        1. Export price > opportunity cost
        2. Import price < opportunity cost
    2. Mutually beneficial: opportunity cost of seller < price < opportunity cost of buyer
8. Marginal analysis
    1. Marginal benefit: 多一个获得的额外好处
    2. Marginal cost: 多一个的成本
    3. If marginal benefit > marginal cost -> continue
9. Utility 效用: a measure of personal satisfaction
    1. Total utility
    2. Marginal utility: slope of total utility, $\cfrac{\Delta \text{Total utility}}{\Delta \text{Quantity}}$
        1. Law of diminishing marginal utility: 随着数量增加，边际效用普遍递减
10. Net benefit: total benefit - total cost
11. Marginal utility per dollar 性价比: $\cfrac{\text{Marginal utility}}{\text{Price}}$
    - 性价比高 -> 多买
    - 性价比低 -> 少买
    - 两种商品: 性价比相同，效用最大，此时考虑尽量花完钱

## Unit 2 - Supply and Demand

### Demand

1. Quantity demanded 需求量: quantity consumers will be willing and able to buy
2. Law of demand 需求定律: price and quantity demanded -> negatively/inversely related
    - The substitution effect 替代品效应: 价格上涨，人们转而购买竞品
    - The income effect 收入效应: 价格下降，购买力增加
3. Demand schedule 需求表: shows price and quantity demanded
4. Demand curve 需求曲线: 纵轴 price, 横轴 quantity demanded
    - Change in quantity demand: 点动
        - Caused by change in price
        - Demand schedule 不变
    - Change in demand: 线动
        - 原因与价格无关
        - Caused by changes in 
            - Prices of
                - Substitutes: substitutes 价格变化，substitutes 的 demand schedule 不变，本产品的变化
                - Complement (如打印机与墨盒、游戏机与游戏盘): complement 价格变化，complement 的 demand schedule 不变，本产品的变化
            - Income
                - Normal good: income increases, demand increases
                - Inferior good: income increases, demand decreases 如公交车票、烟、经济舱
            - Tastes and preference
            - Expectations 对未来物价的预期
            - Number of consumers
5. Market demand: 某售价时，所有人购买力之和 (horizontal sum)

### Supply

1. Quantity supplied: quantity a good or service producers will and able to supply
2. Law of supply 供给定律: price and quantity supplied -> positively related
3. Supply schedule 供给表: shows price and quantity supplied
4. Supply curve 供给曲线: 纵轴 price, 横轴 quantity supplied
    - Change in quantity demand: 点动
        - Caused by change in price
        - Supply schedule 不变
    - Change in supply: 线动
        - 原因与价格无关
        - Caused by changes in
            - Input prices 成本
            - Quantity/quality of input
            - Technology
            - Taxes/subsidies 税收/补贴
            - Expectations 未来涨价，希望在未来供给，因此减少当前的供给
            - Number of sellers
            - Reletive goods
                - Alternative production: alternative 价格上升，则 alternative 的供给增加，本产品供给相应减少
                - Co-product: 正相关

### Elasticity

1. Definition: a measure of the responsiveness of quantity demanded or quantity supplied to a change in one of its determinant
2. Price elasticity of demand (PED): $\left| \cfrac{\text{Percentage change in quantity demanded}}{\text{Percentage change in price}} \right| = \left| \cfrac{(Q_2 - Q_1) / Q_1}{(P_2 - P_1) / P_1} \right|$
    - 若不知道变化方向，则使用 $\left| \cfrac{(Q_2 - Q_1) / \bar{Q}}{(P_2 - P_1) / \bar{P}} \right|$
    - 性质
        - $\text{PED} < 1 \implies \% \Delta Q_\text{d} < \% \Delta P$, demand is **inelastic**
            - $\text{PED} = 0 \implies \% \Delta Q_\text{d} = 0$, demand is **perfectly inelastic**
            - PED 越小，图像越陡 (steeper)
            - $\text{Total Revenue (TR)} = PQ_\text{d}$ 与价格正相关，与需求量负相关
        - $\text{PED} > 1 \implies \% \Delta Q_\text{d} > \% \Delta P$, demand is **elastic**
            - $\text{PED} = \infty \implies \% \Delta P = 0$, demand is **perfectly elastic**
            - PED 越大，图像越平
            - TR 与价格负相关，与需求量正相关
        - $\text{PED} = 1 \implies \% \Delta Q_\text{d} = \% \Delta P$, demand is **unit elastic**
            - TR 恒定
    - Determinant
        - Availability of close substitutes: 如衣服弹性大，药品弹性小
        - Necessities versus luxuries
            - Necessities: inelastic
            - Luxuries: elastic
        - Definition of the market: 竞争有弹性，垄断无弹性
        - Time horizon: 不急有弹性，着急无弹性
        - Proportion of income: 越贵弹性越大
3. Income elasticity of demanded (YED): $\text{YED} = \cfrac{\text{Percentage change in quantity demanded}}{\text{Percentage change in income}}$
    1. 正负
        - Normal goods: $\text{YED} > 0$
        - Inferior goods: $\text{YED} < 0$
    2. 性质
        - Necessities: $0 < \text{YED} < 1$
        - Luxuries: $\text{YED} > 1$
4. Cross-price elasticity of demand (XPED): $\text{XPED} = \cfrac{\text{Percentage change in quantity demanded of good 1}}{\text{Percentage change in price of good 2}}$
    1. 正负
        - Substitutes: $\text{XPED} > 0$
        - Complement: $\text{XPED} < 0$
5. Price elasticity of supply (PES): $\cfrac{\text{Percentage change in quantity supplied}}{\text{Percentage change in price}} = \cfrac{(Q_2 - Q_1) / Q_1}{(P_2 - P_1) / P_1}$
    1. Determinant
        1. Flexibility of production: 如工业产品的产量变化比农产品更灵活，所以 $\text{PES}_\text{工业产品} > \text{PES}_\text{农产品}$
        2. Availability of inputs: 原材料越好获得弹性越高
        3. Production time: 生产时间越短弹性越高
        4. 是否容易储存: 越容易储存弹性越高

### Consumer Surplus and Producer Surplus

1. Consumer surplus
    1. Consumer surplus = value to buyers (willing to pay) - amount paid by buyers (price)
    2. Consumer surplus (at some price) is the area below the demand curve and above the price
2. Producer surplus
    1. Producer surplus = amount received by sellers (price) - cost to sellers = profit
    2. Producer surplus (at some price) is the area below the price and above the supply curve
3. Total surplus
    1. Total surplus = consumer surplus + producer surplus
    2. Total surplus = willing to pay - willing to sell
    3. 平衡时最大？
4. Market efficiency
    1. Archived when the allocation of resources maximizes total surplus 双方 welfare 最大
        - 否则 inefficient
    2. Deadweight loss 无谓损失: 与 maximized total surplus 的差距
5. Government intervention
    1. Price control
        1. Price ceiling
            1. protect buyers
            2. Consequence: price decreased, quantity decreased
            3. 如房价
        2. Price floor
            1. protect suppliers
            2. Consequence: price increased, quantity decreased
            3. 如粮食、工资
    2. Tax and subsidy
        1. Taxes
            1. Taxes on sellers: supply curve 向上移动税收
                - 政府收到的税: $Q_\text{new} \times \Delta P$
            2. 一定会让价格上升，交易量变少，政府获得福利
            3. Tax wedge 买家和卖家谁承担的税更多: 更 inelastic 承担更多
                1. Inelastic 更离不开市场，承担税更多

### International Trade

1. Export and import
2. Government intervention
    1. Tariff
        1. Government 多收钱
        2. 本国生产者多收钱
        3. 消费者福利下降
        4. 产生 deadweight loss
    2. Import quota 配额
        1. 与关税相比，政府仅仅减少收入

## Unit 3 - Production, Cost, and Perfect Competition Model 

### Production Function

1. Production: process that transform factors (input) into goods (output)
    1. Total product (TP)
    2. Marginal product (MP)
    3. Average product (AP)
2. Production function: $Q_\text{output} = f(Q_\text{input})$
3. Measures of cost
    1. Cost function: Total cost = Total fixed costs + Total variable costs
    2. Average total/fixed/variable cost
    3. Marginal cost = $\cfrac{\Delta \text{TC}}{\Delta Q} = \cfrac{\Delta \text{TVC}}{\Delta Q}$
    4. Marginal cost 与 marginal product 图像水平镜像
    5. ATC = MC 时，ATC 最小
        - Efficient scale: 此时的 Q
    6. AVC = MC 时，AVC 最小
4. Shift of product and cost curves
    1. Technology ↑
        1. TM, MP, AP ↑
        2. AVC, ATC, MC ↓
    2. Price of factors of production
        1. Price of fixed input: TFC, AFC, TC, ATC
        2. Price of variable input: TVC, AVC, MC, TC, ATC
    3. Government intervention
        1. Lump-sum 一次性的 tax/subsidy: TFC, AFC, TC, ATC
        2. Per-unit tax/subsidy: TVC, AVC, MC, TC, ATC
5. Long-run cost and economy of scale
    1. In long-run, all fixed inputs become variable inputs
    2. 长期 ATC 是所有短期 ATC 最低点（minimum efficient scale）的集合
    3. Economies of scale: 长期 ATC 随着规模增加而下降（buy in bulk）
        - 一般出现在小规模
        - Increasing returns to scale
    4. Constant returns to scale: 长期 ATC 与规模无关
        - 一般出现在中等规模
        - 成本与产出成比例
    5. Diseconomies of scale: 长期 ATC 随着规模增加而上升
        - 一般出现在大规模
        - Decreasing returns to scale

### Perfectly Competitive Market

1. Revenue
    1. Total revenue (TR) = $P \times Q$
    2. Average revenue (AR) = $\cfrac{\text{TR}}{Q} = P$
    3. Marginal revenue (MR) = $\cfrac{\Delta \text{TR}}{\Delta Q}$
2. Profit
    1. Economic profit = TR - Explicit costs - Implicit costs
    2. Accounting profit = TR - Explicit costs
    3. Normal profit: economic profit = 0
3. Market structures
    1. Monopoly 垄断
        1. Seller entry barriers 高
    2. Perfect competition
        1. Many small firms, many buyers
        2. Identical products (perfect substitutes)
        3. Seller entry barriers 低
        4. Firms are "price takers", who have no control over price
4. Demand for a firm is perfectly elastic
    1. Demand = MR = P
5. Profit maximization rule
    1. MC < MR: 多卖
    2. MC > MR: 少卖
    3. 目标: MC = MR
6. 图像
    1. Market: supply 递增, demand 递减, 交点决定市场价格
    2. Firm
        1. $P_\text{firm} = P_\text{market} = \text{MR} = D$
        2. MC 先减后增
        3. $\text{Total profit} = (P - \text{ATC}) × Q$
7. Shut down and exit
    1. Shut down: short-run decision, 合同未到期
        1. 若 TR > TVC: 亏钱，但还会继续（不看房租可视为赚钱），不会 shut down
        2. 若 TR < TVC: 亏钱，且不会继续，即 shut down
            - P < ATC
    2. Exit: long-run decision, 合同到期
        - 同上，P < ATC 则 exit
8. Long-run equilibrium: zero profit
    1. If firms make prifits
        1. New firms will enter the market, short-run market supply curve shift to right
        2. Price ↓, profits ↓, firms enter ↓ until long-run equilibrium is reached
    2. If firms make loss
        1. Some firms will exit, short-run market supply curve shift to left
        2. Price ↑, profits ↑, firms exit ↓ until long-run equilibrium is reached
    3. 最终 profit = 0, P = MR = MC = ATC
9. Constant/increasing/decreasing cost industry
    1. Constant cost industry
    2. Increasing cost industry: 行业产量越大，行业成本越高，如房地产
    3. Decreasing cost industry: 行业产量越大，行业成本越低，如电商运输成本
10. Efficiency
    1. Allocative efficiency: price = marginal cost, demand = supply
    2. Productive efficiency: firm operates at efficient scale, min ATC = MC, 只在 equilibrium 出现

## Unit 4 - Imperfect Competition

1. Monopoly
    1. 难以进入
    2. 不一定有利润
    3. 可自定价格
    4. Source
        1. Monopoly resources 自然资源
        2. Government-created monopolies
        3. Natural monopolies: 一家公司生产比多家公司生产更有效
            1. Economies of scale
            2. MC < ATC
    5. Profit maximization
        1. Demand curve 递减
        2. Profit = TR- TC
        3. P > ATC: profit
        4. 降价才能多卖 → marginal revenue < price
        5. Profit max: MR = MC
        6. Monopolist operates in the elastic range of demand (where MR > 0)
    6. Efficiency
        1. Allocative efficiency
            1. Demand = marginal cost → allocative efficient
            2. Monopoly 无 allocative efficient, demand > MR - MC
            3. Quantity: MR = MC
            4. 有 Deadweight loss
        2. Productive efficiency
            1. 在 min ATC 生产，min ATC = MC
        3. Socially optimal: MC = P
        4. Fair return: ATC = P
        5. Per unit subsidy: shift MC downward
    7. Regulation
        1. Subsidy
            1. Social optimal
            2. 达到 allocative efficient: 提升 consumer surplus
    8. Price discrimination
        1. Assumption: a monopoly can separate customers into different groups
        2. No comsumer surplus (willing to pay = price)
        3. D = MR = MC: allocative efficient, no deadweight loss
2. Monopolistic competition
    1. Characteristics
        1. Many sellers
        2. Free entry and exit → zero profit
        3. Slightly different
        4. 产量小于 perfect competition，价格大于 perfect competition
3. Oligopoly 寡头
    1. 特征
        1. A few large firms
        2. Identical or differenciated products
        3. High barriers to entry
        4. Control over price
        5. Mutual interdependence
    2. Game theory
    3. Collusion 勾结
        - 目标：joint/combined profit 共同利益最大化
    4. Cartel: a group of firms acting in unison 集团
        1. Set price and output at an agreed upon level
        2. Require identical or highly similar demand and costs
        3. Have a way to punish cheaters
        4. Together at as a monopoly, 因此定价无法超过 monopoly
        5. Price war
        6. Price leadership
        7. Antitrust policy

## Unit 6 - Market Failure and the Role of Government

1. Market faliure: 市场无法合理高效地分配资源
2. Externality 外部性：对第三方的影响
    1. Social cost (SC) = Private cost (PC) + External cost (EC)
        1. Negative externality: SC > EC, 有 external cost
        2. positive 
    2. Social benefit (SB) = Private benefit (PB) + External benefit (EB)
        1. Negative externality: SB < EB, 有 external cost
        2. positive externality: SC > EC
    3. Solutions
        1. Regulation
        2. Market-based policies (internalize the externalily): Tax/subsidies
            1. Corrective tax (negative externalily): 把外部成本转移至内部, Per-unit tax = MEC
            2. Corrective subsidies (positive externalily): 把好处转移给 private sector, Per-unit subsidies = MEB
